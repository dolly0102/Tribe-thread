About TribeThreads

TribeThreads was born from a deep love for African culture, art, and identity. We believe fashion is more than fabric — it’s a language that speaks of pride, unity, and creativity. Every outfit we design tells a story of where we come from and who we aspire to be.

From the bustling streets of Lagos to the vibrant heart of Abeokuta, our journey has always been about connecting tradition with innovation. At TribeThreads, we aim to redefine African fashion for the modern world — bold, beautiful, and proudly rooted.

Our Mission

To celebrate African heritage through sustainable, elegant, and culturally inspired fashion pieces. We are dedicated to empowering local artisans, supporting creativity, and promoting authentic African craftsmanship across the globe.
